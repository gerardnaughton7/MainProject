UniBooks Welcome to UniBooks

UniBooks is a App I created for University/College Students.

With the App Students can find books for their courses and contact the seller in order to buy. Also Students can sell/exchange their old books to other students

To start browsing for books visit the books tab right away.

To sell a book tap the menu tab in the top left corner and follow the links to either Log in or Sign up. After that is completed tap the Create Ad Tab at the bottom of screen.
